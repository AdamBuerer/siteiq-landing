Four AI Agents. One Closed Loop. Fully Automated.

Four specialized AI agents operate as a closed-loop system that continuously: observe → interpret → decide → execute → communicate. No manual coordination. No fragmented tools.

01
Capture Agent

Continuously ingests data from robots, IoT sensors, and building systems — keeping the digital twin accurate and always current.

02
Application Agent

Transforms raw building data into operational intelligence: cost quantification, risk assessment, and performance gap identification.

03
Orchestration Agent

Turns decisions into real-world execution — dispatching robots, managing work orders, and optimizing across energy, cleaning, and maintenance simultaneously.

04
Interaction Agent

Connects the system to the humans who use it. Delivers AI insights through GenUIX — a natural, role-aware interface that adapts to each user.
